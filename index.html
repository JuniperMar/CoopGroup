About

Coop group leads the upkeep of Cleopatra's, our resident hen's, enclosure and coop area. Although she is 5 years old now and not laying eggs anymore, Cleo still appreciates the care and attention she receive from students. It's also a great way to make friends and learn some interesting things about caring for animals. Come join us!
